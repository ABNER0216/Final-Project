Using Rules

  • This system is only for use by teachers and students in school, and other irrelevant activities are not allowed in the comprehensive gymnasium. Please use Xiamen University Malaysia Unified Identity Authentication to log in to this system.

  • The opening time of the comprehensive gymnasium is 09:00-22:00, and the venue is cleared at 21:30. The booking time should not be during the closing time of the stadium, otherwise you will not be able to use the stadium due to the closing time, and you should leave the stadium before 21:30.

  • After successful reservation, the booking personnel must arrive at the registration office of the booking venue on time at the beginning of the booking time to complete the registration procedures.

  • Each student only allowed to add three classes at most.

  • Contact the coach if student can't attend class on time for personal reason.

  • Before leaving, please make sure to do the following:


    1.All sports facilities are in good condition and put back, consciously turn off the power of fitness equipment;
    2.Turn off air conditioning and light in the room; The doors and Windows of the room have been closed and locked.
    3.Bring your own belongings and take the garbage away from the venue. Failure to do so will be considered a violation.


    Please feel free to contact the staff if you have any questions.
  • Please be familiar with the safety instructions for the use of the comprehensive gymnasium, and consciously abide by the relevant regulations on the use of the comprehensive gymnasium. Once the violation is found, the violators will be restricted from using the gymnasium again.
